## 👋 About Me

I'm **Stanislav Troshin** — a DevOps engineer focused on building scalable, reliable infrastructure and automating away operational complexity.

My journey includes studying **Software Engineering** at BSUIR (Belarusian State University of Informatics and Radioelectronics), with a strong foundation in physics and mathematics from Gymnasium №39 in Minsk.
